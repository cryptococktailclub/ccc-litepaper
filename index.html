Ownership for Cocktail Culture

A system that lets bartenders create, bars serve, and guests participate.

The Problem

Cocktail culture generates enduring economic and cultural value, yet ownership remains fragmented and informal. Recipes circulate freely, venues capture episodic upside, and the creators responsible for differentiation rarely participate beyond wages and tips.

Mission

Crypto Cocktail Club exists to establish ownership infrastructure for cocktail culture—aligning incentives without introducing speculative dynamics, operational burden, or consumer friction.

Cocktail NFT Component

Bartenders mint original cocktail recipes as programmable NFTs through the Crypto Cocktail Club dApp. Each NFT represents a single-use execution credential.

Venues opt in to specific Cocktail NFTs by signaling that they possess the ingredients, equipment, and operational capability to execute the drink.

Patrons purchase the NFT and select a participating venue at the time of redemption. Once redeemed, the NFT is burned, preventing reuse and ensuring attribution integrity.

TIPZ Incentive Coordination

TIPZ functions as an incentive coordination layer, not a consumer payment rail. Allocation occurs automatically at redemption, reinforcing high-quality execution and participation.

Compliance & Regulatory Posture

Crypto Cocktail Club does not custody user funds, process consumer payments, or require crypto usage at point of service.

Cocktail NFTs function as access and execution credentials. TIPZ operates as an incentive mechanism, not a speculative asset or consumer payment system.

Settlement into stable operational rails occurs off-chain where appropriate, preserving regulatory alignment and minimizing operational friction.

Founder Perspective

Crypto Cocktail Club was born from a decade behind the bar— witnessing first-hand how cultural value is created long before it is captured.

The protocol exists to ensure bartenders participate in the value they generate, and to establish equitable terms before corporate consolidation dictates them.
