Ownership Infrastructure for Cocktail Culture

Designed for bars, not traders.

Crypto Cocktail Club introduces programmable access, real-world redemption, and incentive alignment to hospitality—so the people who create culture participate in its value.

The Problem

Cocktail culture generates enduring economic and cultural value, yet the people who create it rarely participate in the upside. Recipes travel freely. Venues profit episodically. Loyalty systems reward consumption, not contribution.

Mission

Crypto Cocktail Club exists to realign cocktail culture so creators, venues, and members all participate in its value—transparently, sustainably, and without speculation-first incentives.

Strategy

CCC approaches hospitality as infrastructure—combining access control, intellectual property, and real-world execution.

  • Membership NFTs — programmable access credentials
  • Redeemable Cocktail IP — recipes with real-world use
  • Venue Network — monetization beyond the four walls

Value Creation Loop

Crypto Cocktail Club treats hospitality as a coordinated system. Cultural value is created, activated, and reinforced across four actors.

Bartenders Create recipes & culture
→

→

→

TIPZ Token Flow

TIPZ is earned through participation and circulates between creators, venues, members, and the protocol treasury. It is not designed as a consumer payment rail.

  • Creators & Bartenders — 30%
  • Venue Network — 25%
  • Member Incentives — 20%
  • Protocol Treasury — 25%
Membership NFT → Access → Venue Redemption → TIPZ Incentives
