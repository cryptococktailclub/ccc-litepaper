Crypto Cocktail Club
Virtual Bar Whitepaper

Ownership for Cocktail Culture

A system that lets bartenders create, bars serve, and guests participate.

The Problem

Cocktail culture generates enduring economic and cultural value, yet ownership remains fragmented and informal. Recipes circulate freely, venues capture episodic upside, and the creators responsible for differentiation rarely participate beyond wages and tips.

Mission

Crypto Cocktail Club exists to establish ownership infrastructure for cocktail culture—aligning incentives without introducing speculative dynamics, operational burden, or consumer friction.

Protocol Execution Flow

Creator Mints Cocktail NFT
↓
Venues Opt In to Execute Recipe
↓
Patron Purchases NFT
↓
Patron Selects Participating Venue
↓
Cocktail Redeemed at Venue
↓
NFT Burned
↓
TIPZ Allocated to Creator, Venue, Protocol

This execution loop mirrors existing hospitality behavior while introducing verifiable attribution, single-use execution, and automated incentive settlement.

Cocktail NFT Component

Bartenders mint original cocktail recipes as programmable NFTs through the Crypto Cocktail Club dApp.

Venues opt in to specific Cocktail NFTs by signaling execution capability.

Patrons purchase the NFT and select a participating venue at the time of redemption. Once redeemed, the NFT is burned.

TIPZ Incentive Coordination

TIPZ functions as an incentive coordination layer, not a consumer payment rail.

Compliance & Regulatory Posture

Crypto Cocktail Club does not custody user funds, process consumer payments, or require crypto usage at point of service.

Founder Perspective

Crypto Cocktail Club was born from a decade behind the bar.

Now is the time to implement a protocol that realigns cocktail culture to be more equitable for the bar professionals who create the craft, carry the culture, and make the industry work night after night.
