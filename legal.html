Legal & Protocol Framework

Rights architecture, attestations, and regulatory posture

Executive Summary

Crypto Cocktail Club (“CCC”) operates a cultural IP registry and activation protocol for cocktail recipes. Recipes are minted once as canonical registry records and are designed for broad real-world use across participating venues.

Canonical NFT Model

Each recipe is registered as a single NFT. There are no editions, no artificial scarcity mechanics, and no speculative supply constraints.

Separation of Rights

  • Authorship: Permanent, non-transferable attribution
  • Usage: Open activation by participating venues
  • Economic Rights: Transferable monetization rights

Economic Rights Marketplace

Creators may sell economic rights through the CCC marketplace. CCC retains a flat 10% fee on all rights ownership sales and resales.

Creator Attestations

Creators attest to originality or lawful rights to publish at mint time. Attestations persist permanently and survive any transfer of economic rights.

Regulatory Posture

CCC does not sell alcohol, custody consumer funds, or offer investment products. Recipe rights are functional IP interests, not securities.
